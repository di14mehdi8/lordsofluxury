How It Works

From Dream to Reality

A three step process. Transparent, considered, and crafted with care at every stage.

01

Share Your Story

We begin with a private consultation at our Bellevue showroom. Tell us about your vision, inspiration, preferred style, metal, and stone. There are no wrong answers. This is your story.

02

3D Design & Preview

Using 3D design technology, we render your ideas into a high resolution digital model. Review every detail, including stone placement, proportions, and metalwork, and refine until it is right.

03

Wear Your Masterpiece

Once approved, we combine precision 3D printing with traditional hand finishing to craft your piece. The result is a treasure as singular as the story behind it.
